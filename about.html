more about me

I am a software developer with proficiency in Ruby on Rails, SQL, HTML, CSS, Javascript, Heroku, AWS, Azure, and many other related technologies.

Additional familiarity with cyber security, data analytics and blockchain development.

After High School I went to Pepperdine University in Malibu California on a music sholarship for classical piano performance.

One year in I decided this was not what I wanted to do and left to pursue music outside of school, I have subsequently toured the world and released many albums.

I began working in the auction industry around 2009 where I initially handled logistics and later became a property specialist, primarily for furniture and decorative arts and later further specializing in musical instruments.

In more recent years I personally handled many large estates of famous musicians such as Walter Becker, Ronnie James Dio, Greg Lake, and Keith Emmerson.

I had been contemplating a transition into tech for some time and in 2020 began the journey in earnest. I completed a cyber security bootcamp at UCLA extension with much success but immediately knew I wanted to further my knowledge and began learning fullstack development in addition to general computer science.
